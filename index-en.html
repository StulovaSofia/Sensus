About us

Sensus is a dating agency for people who have not found love yet, but are ready for a relationship and, possibly, to start a family in the future. Looking for love is so natural, but so difficult in today's world, that's why we, Sensus matchmakers, accompany our clients through all the important stages on their way to building a happy relationship.

We help conserve one of the most important assets in the modern world - time, so that our clients can avoid disappointment from endless, meaningless matches in dating apps that rarely lead to healthy relationships.

Our methods

Our matchmaking methods are based on psychological principles, professional advice and the willingness to get to know our clients closely in order to help them meet their soulmate with whom they can go through life. We carefully select a couple for our clients, making sure that the two parties share similar values, ideals and interests.
