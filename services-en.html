Programs and services

You meet thousands of people who don’t connect with you. And then you meet that one special person, and your life is changed. Forever.

Our programs

01Standard program

If you want to browse profiles on your own and actively participate in searches, then our Standard Program is perfect for you. The matchmaker acts as an intermediary and connects you with the person with whom you would like to chat and only with mutual consent you exchange contacts. Besides, your matchmaker is always ready to help arrange dates and answer all your questions.

As part of the Standard Program, we offer:

• Access to a closed client base
• Personal matchmaker’s communication and support

Program duration:

Program duration: 3, 6 and 12 months or 1 month with a monthly extension. You can upgrade to the Premium or Customized program at any time.

1 month: 8 000 ₽

3 months: 15 000 ₽

6 months: 25 000 ₽

02Premium program

As part of the Premium program, the matchmaker selects suitable candidates according to your preferences and presents them to you. After the first conversation, within 3 calendar days, the consultant contacts and reports on the progress of the search. Within 7-10 days from the moment of signing the contract, we present to you at least one candidate. The search within the Premium Program will last as long as it is necessary for you to meet the right person.

As part of the Standard Program, we offer:

• Selection of potential partners according to your criteria
• Daily communication and support of the matchmaker
• Assistance in arranging dates
• Language support
• Psychological support - online or offline session with our psychologist-coach once a quarter.
• Arranging photo shoots

Program duration:

3, 6 and 12 months. The minimum period of the Premium Program should be at least 3 months, as some reasonable time is needed for a thorough selection.

3 months: 40 000 ₽

6 months: 70 000 ₽

12 months: 130 000 ₽

03 Customized program

The basis of the Customized program is the same as the Premium program - the matchmaker not only selects potential partners for you based on your criteria and values, but he/she is in touch with you 24 hours. You are also provided with 1 coaching/psychological session, sending a surprise gift, translation services, car rent, a tourist excursion, a romantic date, and a photo shoot.

As part of the Customized Program, we offer:

• Selection of potential partners according to your criteria
• Matchmaker communication and support 24/7
• Assistance in arranging dates
• Language support
• 1 psychological/coaching session per month
• Organization of a photo shoot
• Sending a surprise gift
• Translation services
• Car rent
• Tourist tour
• The ability to change the criteria for the partners selection during the entire work with the matchmaker

Program duration:

3, 6 и 12 месяцев.

3 months: 70 000 ₽

6 months: 130 000 ₽

12 months: 250 000 ₽
